History is a mirror held between civilizations. In each reflection, we find not difference alone, but the shared human impulse to create, to lead, to understand, and to endure.

历史是文明之间的一面镜子。在每一个倒影中，我们发现的不仅是差异，更是人类共同的创造、领导、理解和坚持的冲动。
